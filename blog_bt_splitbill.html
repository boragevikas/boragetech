The Story of BT SplitBill: Building Privacy-First Digital Courage.

Vikas Borage March 21, 2026 7 min read

1. The Concept: "Minimalist Utility"

The core philosophy of BT-SplitBill is to remove the "friction" from shared finances.

The Problem: Most expense-splitting apps are bloated with social features, intrusive ads, or complex onboarding that confuses casual users.

The Solution: A high-speed, logic-driven utility that does one thing perfectly: calculates who owes what. It’s built for users who want to log an expense in 5 seconds and get back to their lives.

2. Core Functionalities (The "What")

We've designed BT SplitBill around real-world scenarios. Here’s how it empowers you:

  • Seamless Group Management: Create groups for roommates, trips, or dinners. Members are added via a simple link or invite, keeping the entry barrier low.
  • Intelligent Expense Logging: Add expenses, specify who paid, and choose between equal or custom splits. The app handles the math instantly.
  • Real-Time "Settle Up" Dashboard: A clear, transparent view of balances. It answers the question "Who do I owe?" without needing to scroll through weeks of history.
  • Cross-Platform Sync: Because it’s built on Flutter, the experience is identical whether you are on the Android App or using the Web App at splitbill.boragetech.in.
  • Instant Notifications: Using Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM), users get notified the moment they are added to a group or a new bill is logged, ensuring no one is left in the dark.

3. The Technical Stack (The "How")

For the fellow developers out there, here is a look at the modern stack powering this experience:

Component Technology Benefit
Frontend Flutter Single codebase for Android and Web; high-performance UI.
Database Cloud Firestore NoSQL real-time syncing across all devices.
Authentication Firebase Auth Secure, one-tap login for user privacy.
Hosting Firebase Hosting Fast global delivery for the web platform.
Monetization AdMob / AdSense A "Zero Cost" model for the developer and user.

4. Design Philosophy: The BorageTech Edge

Why does BT-SplitBill feel different? It’s intentional:

  • Minimalist UI: No unnecessary animations or "extra" buttons. Every pixel has a purpose.
  • Privacy-First: Unlike many free tools, BT-SplitBill is built by a developer who values data privacy. No unauthorized tracking or data selling.
  • Offline-Ready: Basic functionality remains snappy even on slower networks, thanks to Flutter’s local caching and Firestore's offline capabilities.

5. Deployment & Sustainability

The "Zero-Cost" Architecture: We've leveraged Google’s Cloud Infrastructure and AdMob to keep the app free for everyone forever. This ensures long-term sustainability without charging users a subscription.

Future Roadmap: We're not stopping here. Upcoming features include PDF report generation for business trips and deep integration with other BorageTech utility tools.
